Athletic Strength & Conditioning Guide
========================================

WARM-UP PROTOCOLS
-----------------
Dynamic Warm-up (10-15 minutes):
1. Light jog: 2-3 minutes
2. High knees: 20 yards x 2
3. Butt kicks: 20 yards x 2
4. Leg swings: 10 each leg (front/back, side/side)
5. Walking lunges: 10 each leg
6. Lateral shuffles: 20 yards each direction
7. Carioca: 20 yards each direction
8. A-skips and B-skips: 20 yards each
9. Sprint buildups: 3 x 40 yards at 50%, 75%, 90%

Never skip the warm-up. Cold muscles are injury-prone muscles.

CORE STRENGTH
-------------
The core is the foundation of all athletic movement.

Essential Core Exercises:
1. Plank: Hold 30-60 seconds, 3 sets
2. Side Plank: 30 seconds each side, 3 sets
3. Dead Bug: 10 reps each side, 3 sets
4. Bird Dog: 10 reps each side, 3 sets
5. Hollow Body Hold: 30 seconds, 3 sets
6. Russian Twists: 20 reps, 3 sets

Advanced Core Work:
- Ab wheel rollouts
- Hanging leg raises
- Medicine ball throws
- Pallof press
- Anti-rotation exercises

LOWER BODY STRENGTH
-------------------
Key Exercises for Athletes:
1. Squats: The king of lower body exercises
   - Keep chest up, knees tracking over toes
   - Go to parallel or below
   - 3-4 sets of 6-10 reps

2. Romanian Deadlifts: Hamstring and glute development
   - Hinge at hips, slight knee bend
   - Feel stretch in hamstrings
   - 3 sets of 8-12 reps

3. Lunges: Unilateral leg strength
   - Forward, reverse, and lateral variations
   - Keep front knee over ankle
   - 3 sets of 10 each leg

4. Box Jumps: Explosive power
   - Land softly with bent knees
   - Step down, don't jump down
   - 3 sets of 5-8 reps

5. Single Leg Squats: Balance and stability
   - Progress from assisted to free
   - 3 sets of 6-8 each leg

UPPER BODY STRENGTH
-------------------
Push Exercises:
1. Push-ups: Master these first
2. Bench Press: Compound chest movement
3. Overhead Press: Shoulder strength
4. Dips: Triceps and chest

Pull Exercises:
1. Pull-ups: Bodyweight back strength
2. Rows: All variations beneficial
3. Face Pulls: Shoulder health
4. Lat Pulldowns: If can't do pull-ups yet

SPEED DEVELOPMENT
-----------------
Sprint Mechanics:
- Drive knees up, not out
- Arm action: cheek to cheek (face to hip)
- Stay on balls of feet
- Powerful ground contact
- Relaxed upper body

Sprint Workouts:
- 10-yard sprints: 8-10 reps (acceleration focus)
- 40-yard sprints: 4-6 reps (speed endurance)
- Flying sprints: 20 yards build-up, 20 yards max speed

AGILITY TRAINING
----------------
Ladder Drills:
- Quick feet patterns
- 2 feet in each box
- Lateral movements
- Ickey shuffle

Cone Drills:
- 5-10-5 shuttle
- T-drill
- Box drill
- L-drill

Change of Direction:
- Plant and cut
- Low center of gravity
- Explosive first step
- Deceleration is key

RECOVERY
--------
Post-Workout:
- Static stretching: Hold each stretch 30 seconds
- Foam rolling: Major muscle groups
- Hydration: Replace fluids lost
- Nutrition: Protein within 30 minutes

Rest and Sleep:
- 7-9 hours of sleep for optimal recovery
- Rest days are when you get stronger
- Listen to your body
- Active recovery on off days

SAMPLE WEEKLY SCHEDULE
----------------------
Monday: Lower body strength + Core
Tuesday: Speed and agility + Sport practice
Wednesday: Upper body strength + Core
Thursday: Sport practice (active recovery focus)
Friday: Full body power + Speed work
Saturday: Game/Competition
Sunday: Complete rest or light activity
