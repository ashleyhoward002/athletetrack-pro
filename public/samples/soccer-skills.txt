Soccer Skills Development Guide
================================

BALL CONTROL
------------
First Touch Principles:
- Cushion the ball to take pace off
- Control with the inside of foot for accuracy
- Keep the ball close to your body
- Look up immediately after control

Receiving on the Move:
- Open your body to see the field
- Let the ball run across your body
- Use the outside of foot when moving quickly
- Always know where you're going before receiving

DRIBBLING TECHNIQUES
--------------------
Close Control:
- Small, quick touches
- Ball stays within playing distance
- Use all surfaces of the foot
- Keep your head up to read the game

Speed Dribbling:
- Push the ball into space ahead
- Use laces for longer touches
- Sprint to the ball
- Best when you have open space

1v1 Moves:
1. Step-over: Fake one direction, go the other
2. Scissors: Circle foot around the ball
3. Cruyff Turn: Fake shot, pull ball behind standing leg
4. Elastico: Quick inside-outside touch
5. La Croqueta: Ball between feet rapidly

PASSING
-------
Short Passing:
- Inside of foot for accuracy
- Plant foot pointing at target
- Follow through toward target
- Proper weight is crucial

Long Passing:
- Strike through the center of ball for drive
- Strike underneath for loft
- Lock ankle firmly
- Full follow through

Through Balls:
- Weight the pass for teammate's run
- Play into space, not to feet
- Timing is everything
- Disguise your intentions

SHOOTING
--------
Power Shots:
- Approach at slight angle
- Plant foot beside the ball
- Strike with laces
- Lock ankle, toe down
- Follow through across body

Placement:
- Inside foot for accuracy
- Pick your corner early
- Low and hard usually beats keeper
- Opposite corner from goalkeeper position

DEFENDING
---------
Individual Defending:
- Approach at angle to force one direction
- Stay on your feet as long as possible
- Jockey and delay
- Don't dive in

Tackling:
- Only when certain of winning the ball
- Go through the ball, not the player
- Stay low, use body weight
- Win it cleanly

HEADING
-------
Attacking Headers:
- Eyes open, watch the ball
- Head through the top half of ball
- Use your forehead
- Generate power from neck and core

Defensive Headers:
- Get height and distance
- Head the ball high and wide
- Attack the ball, don't wait for it

PRACTICE ROUTINES
-----------------
Technical Warm-up (15 min):
- Juggling: 100 touches
- Wall passing: 50 each foot
- Cone dribbling: 5 minutes
- First touch exercises: 5 minutes

Daily Ball Mastery (20 min):
- Toe taps: 60 seconds
- Inside-outside: 60 seconds each foot
- Sole rolls: 60 seconds each foot
- Pull-push: 60 seconds each direction
- L-turns: 30 each foot
- V-pulls: 30 each foot

Shooting Drill:
Set up 5 balls at edge of box. Shoot each one, focusing on placement first. Gradually increase power while maintaining accuracy.
